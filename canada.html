What is Canada doing?

Climate Change is a massive issue that is slowly starting to affect everyone around the world. At the moment, Canada is leading the world race in climate change action. From the CAF (CLimate Action fund)to the carbon tax rules. Canada has done a great deal of work in order to make sure that they are leading the race in the protection of our planet.

Above is a graph, measured in tonnes, which demonstrates Canada's country impact on on the cumultive warming of the planet per province. Ontario being the most urbanized province in Canada, leads the race with over a ton of C02 emmisions and other harmful gasses being released into the atmosphere.
